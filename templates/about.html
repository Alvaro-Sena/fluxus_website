About Us

Welcome to Casa Fluxus! We are a collaborative and eco-friendly nomadic base, offering accommodation in exchange for volunteer work such as cooking, helping with activities, and collaborating on environmental preservation projects. We host community activities, like sewing cycles and Portuguese language classes, with a focus on collective well-being and non-profit values. Come explore our space and projects, contribute your energy, and share experiences with us!
